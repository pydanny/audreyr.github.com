Pushing the limits of color and texture.

Digital Rainbows: 2007-2010

In 2007, I became obsessed with rainbows and clouds.

I was getting ready for Mission Open Studios 2007, painting landscapes for the show. At some point, I realized that I could write a computer program to automate the process, so I did.

Rainbows: Series 1

Series 1 consists of my early rainbows.

Rainbows: Series 1.5

Series 1.5 consists of developments in the structure of my rainbows. Some of these pieces do not even contain rainbows.

Rainbows: Series 2

Series 2 shows an evolution of my generative art techniques and refinement of my printing process.
